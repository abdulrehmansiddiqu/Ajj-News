Shoib Malik ties the knote for a second time

He and first wife Sania Mirza almost a year ago

Updated 20 Jan, 2024 PAKISTAN

The cricketer broke the news to his fans on his Instagram and X accounts. PHOTO X@realshoaibmalik

Pakistani cricketer Shoaib Malik has tied the knot for a second time, over a year after reports claimed he had separated from his first wife Sania Mirza.

Actress Sana Javed is Malik’s new wife.
The cricketer broke the news to his fans on his Instagram and X accounts on Saturday morning.
He shared two photographs showing Malik and Sana Javed in a half-embrace with a broad coyish smile on the bride’s face.
Malik thanked God by saying “Alhamdulillah” and added a verse from the Holy Quran with its English translation “And We created you in pairs”.
